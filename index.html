Our Process

How ELS911 Works

A structured, proven engagement model that gets your school mapped, certified, and integrated with first responders — with minimal disruption to your school day.

1

Initial Consultation

We meet with your administration to understand your facility, existing safety plans, local emergency contacts, and compliance requirements.

2

On-Site Assessment & LiDAR Scan

Our assessors visit your facility and conduct a complete walkthrough, capturing every space with precision LiDAR scanning technology.

3

Floor Plan & 3D Model Production

Point cloud data is processed into precise 2D floor plans and 3D dollhouse models — annotated for emergency use with entrances, exits, utility shutoffs, and room IDs.

4

Emergency Plan Development

Working with your team and local first responders, we develop a comprehensive emergency response plan tied directly to your facility maps.

5

Certification & Training

Staff are trained on the plan. Administration receives official certification documentation. First responders receive pre-loaded access to your floor plans.

6

Annual Renewal & SOC 2 Recertification

Annual reviews keep your certification current and your community protected. Includes independent SOC 2 Third Party recertification every year — not just once.
