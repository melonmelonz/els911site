ELS911 Services

Comprehensive School Safety Solutions

From initial site assessment through ongoing support, ELS911 delivers the tools and documentation that keep your school community safe and your administration prepared.

Facility Mapping

Precise, accurate 2D floor plans of every room, corridor, entrance, and utility shutoff — formatted for first responder use and digital distribution.

Emergency Response Planning

Structured, actionable emergency plans developed with your administration and aligned with local law enforcement and fire department protocols.

Staff Certification

Training and certification programs for administrators and staff so your team is confident and prepared — not just compliant on paper.

Site Assessment

On-site evaluation of vulnerabilities, access points, and security gaps, with a prioritised remediation report tailored to your facility.

First Responder Integration

School Infrastructure

Your building is critical infrastructure

ELS911 treats your school facility the same way engineers treat bridges and power grids — as infrastructure that must be documented, maintained, and accessible to responders at any moment. A school without a current, accurate floor plan is infrastructure without a blueprint.

Our Process

How ELS911 Works

A structured, proven engagement model that gets your school mapped, certified, and integrated with first responders — with minimal disruption to your school day.

1

Initial Consultation

We meet with your administration to understand your facility, existing safety plans, local emergency contacts, and compliance requirements. No obligation — just clarity.

2

On-Site Assessment & LiDAR Scan

Our assessors visit your facility and conduct a complete walkthrough, capturing every space with precision LiDAR scanning technology to produce accurate, detailed floor plans.

3

Floor Plan Production

Point cloud data is processed into precise 2D floor plans annotated for emergency use — entrances, exits, utility shutoffs, rally points, and room IDs all clearly marked.

4

Emergency Plan Development

Working with your team and local first responders, we develop a comprehensive emergency response plan tied directly to your facility maps.

5

Certification & Training

Staff are trained on the plan. Administration receives official certification documentation. First responders receive pre-loaded access to your floor plans.

6

Ongoing Updates & Support

As your facility changes, we update your maps and plans. Annual reviews keep your certification current and your community protected.

Uniform K-12 Mapping Act

The Uniform K-12 Mapping Act requires schools to maintain accurate, standardised facility maps accessible to emergency responders. ELS911 was built specifically to help schools achieve and maintain full compliance — efficiently, affordably, and without the guesswork.
